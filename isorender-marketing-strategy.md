# IsoRender GTM Strategy: Photo → 3D Walkthrough

## Executive Summary
Position IsoRender as the UK's most accessible "photo-to-3D" tool for property professionals — cheaper and faster than Matterport, easier than manual 3D建模.

---

## 1. Target Customer Segments

### Primary Segments

| Segment | Size (UK) | Pain Points | Willingness to Pay |
|---------|-----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Estate Agents** | ~20k agents | Costly virtual tours (£50-100/mo Matterport), slow turnaround | £20-50/mo |
| **Property Developers** | ~5k firms | Can't visualise off-plan, need marketing assets fast | £50-100/mo |
| **Architects** | ~35k registered | Client presentations, planning submissions | £30-80/mo |
| **Interior Designers** | ~15k | Renovation visualisation, client sign-off | £20-40/mo |

### Secondary Segments
- **Homeowners** (DIY renovations) — Lower ARPU, higher volume
- **Airbnb/Letting agents** — Quick property showcases
